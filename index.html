- The Trans Justice Funding Project

To order, fill out this form and venmo @Usha-Bhalla or @Naina-Bhalla the quantity you wish to donate.

We can ship the posters (but please take into account the $1 shipping fee), or give them to you in person in AZ or if you go to Dartmouth or Brown. If you wish to have them shipped, please use the form to order.

If you have any questions or concerns, please email either of us at usha_bhalla@brown.edu or naina.bhalla.22@dartmouth.edu or any other way that you know how to contact us!

Please note that buying these posters does not make you antiracist or absolve you of any further responsibility you might have in being antiracist. Please continue to educate yourself, donate what you can, whether that be time, money, or energy, and work on being more actively antiracist.

Thank you!
